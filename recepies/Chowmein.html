Chow Mein Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces chow mein noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the chow mein noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, and stir-fry for 2 minutes until fragrant and slightly softened.
  4. Add the carrot, bell pepper, cabbage, and bean sprouts. Stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the cooked chow mein noodles to the skillet and pour the sauce over them.
  7. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through, about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot. Garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if desired.
