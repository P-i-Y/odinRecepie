Momos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup water
  • For the filling:
    • 1 pound ground meat (chicken, pork, or lamb)
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
    • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt for the dough. Gradually add water and knead until a smooth dough is formed. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix all the ingredients for the filling until well combined.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a thin circle.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each dough circle.
  5. Fold the dough over the filling to create a half-moon shape. Pinch the edges to seal the momo.
  6. Repeat the process for the remaining dough and filling.
  7. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
