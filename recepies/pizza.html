Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  6. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  7. Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal parts.
  8. Roll out each portion into a round pizza crust of desired thickness.
  9. Place the pizza crusts on baking sheets or pizza stones.
  10. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the crusts, leaving a small border around the edges.
  11. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  12. Add your favorite toppings.
  13. Bake the pizzas in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  14. Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
